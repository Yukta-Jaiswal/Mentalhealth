Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.Conditions such as stress, depression, and anxiety can all affect mental health and disrupt a person’s routine.

Steps to improve your mental health

It’s important to take care of yourself and get the most from life. Below are 10 practical ways to look after your mental health. Making simple changes to how you live doesn’t need to cost a fortune or take up loads of time. Anyone can follow this advice. Why not start today?

Talk about your feelings

Talking about your feelings can help you stay in good mental health and deal with times when you feel troubled.

Take a break

A change of scene or a change of pace is good for your mental health.It could be a five-minute pause from cleaning your kitchen, a half-hour lunch break at work, or a weekend exploring somewhere new.

Accept who you are

It’s much healthier to accept that you’re unique than to wish you were more like someone else. Good self-esteem helps you cope when life takes a difficult turn.

Write down something you are grateful for.

Gratitude has been clearly linked with improved well-being and mental health, as well as happiness.Find something to be grateful for, let it fill your heart, and bask in that feeling.

Improve your physical health.

Improving your diet and exercising regularly are proven to give your mental health and confidence a boost.
